Website Improvement Pointers

High Priority

Add Real Portfolio Content

The current site's portfolio page appears empty. Showcase 6-10 best projects with high-quality thumbnails, short descriptions, and embedded video. This is the #1 thing that converts visitors to clients. Use the mosaic grid concept to display them.

High Priority

Clear Service Descriptions

List every service with a brief description of what's included, who it's for, and a starting price range or "request a quote" CTA. Visitors should immediately understand what you offer and how to hire you.

High Priority

Stronger Call-to-Action

Add a prominent "Get a Quote" or "Book a Consultation" button in the hero, after every section, and in a sticky nav. Make it dead simple for someone to reach out. Include a contact form, not just an email link.

High Priority

Social Proof & Testimonials

Add client testimonials, logos of companies worked with, and stats (projects completed, years in business). Social proof builds trust instantly, especially for a service-based business.

Medium Priority

SEO & Local Presence

Target "AV production DMV", "video production DC", "event videographer Maryland" etc. Add location-specific content, a Google Business profile, and structured data markup. Most clients search locally.

Medium Priority

Demo Reel / Video Hero

As an AV company, your website MUST showcase your work immediately. Replace the static hero with a 30-60 second demo reel playing in the background or as a prominent embedded video. Show, don't tell.

Medium Priority

Upgrade from Generic Theme

The current Neve/WordPress theme is functional but generic. Consider a custom-built site (or premium theme customization) that matches the creative quality of the work you produce. Your website IS your first portfolio piece.

Medium Priority

Speed & Mobile Optimization

Ensure fast load times (under 3 seconds), especially on mobile. Compress images, use lazy loading, and test on real phones. Over 60% of visitors will come from mobile devices.
